DELICIOUS CAKES AND PASTRY

At It’s Delicious, we know desserts should always be made from scratch, with love. Every cake, cupcake or confection that leaves our bakery is made with the finest natural ingredients using Pastry Chef Cristal Paredes’ exclusive recipes. For us, what we put into our cakes is just as important as what we leave out. Everything you’ll ever taste from It’s Delicious is made without the use of artificial flavors or pre-made mixes. We believe the best cakes in the world are as delicious as they are beautiful, but most importantly, they should be made just for you.
